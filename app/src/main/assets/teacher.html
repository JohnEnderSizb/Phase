Other Features For Teachers

For the convenience of students, double clicking on any word in a story will bring up a dictionary definition along with an audio pronunciation of the word.

About The Teaching Materials

We are in the process of building up an online resource of teaching materials for use with short stories. Materials include comprehension questions and ideas for discussion or accompanying activities and games. All materials are produced by qualified teachers.

Materials are presented with a suggested age range for those studying English as a first language and an indication of the language level for those studying English as an additional language.

To access the materials, use the list above.
