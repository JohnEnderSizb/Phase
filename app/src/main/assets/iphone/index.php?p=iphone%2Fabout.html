East of the Web is one of the world's leading electronic publishers of short stories.

Founded in 2000, eastoftheweb.com has built up an international reputation as a destination for people looking for a good, short read. As well as providing one of the largest selections of classic and modern stories by established authors, East of the Web is a great place to discover new and up-and-coming talent.

With the release of the Short Stories app for iPhone, iPod touch and iPad, East of the Web is excited to bring the same excellence to Apple's mobile devices.
